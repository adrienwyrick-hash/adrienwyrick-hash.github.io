Account & Data

How do I delete my account?

Open WS Drift → Main Menu → Options → Privacy section → tap DELETE ACCOUNT. A single tap permanently removes your sign-in identity, best score, daily streak, achievement progress, and all preferences stored on the device. This action is immediate and cannot be undone.

What data does WS Drift store?

Only what's needed to play: your best score, settings, achievement progress, and optionally your Sign in with Apple identifier. No data is sent to our servers — WS Drift doesn't have a server. Game Center scores are managed by Apple.

Purchases

How do I restore my purchases?

Open WS Drift → Hangar (Ship Select) → tap the Restore button at the bottom. Sign into the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

I bought Remove Ads but I still see ads

1. Confirm the purchase in Settings → [Your Name] → Media & Purchases → Purchase History.
2. In WS Drift, open the Hangar and tap Restore.
3. Force quit and relaunch the app.
4. If ads still appear, email us with your Apple ID purchase receipt.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple. Request one at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Gameplay

How do controls work?

Drag your thumb anywhere on the screen to drift your fighter. Your bolts fire on their own — focus on positioning and dodging.

What happens if I miss a wave?

Each wave has a 15-second timeout. If you can't kill every ship, the wave still ends so your round can progress.

Where do my scores save?

Your personal best saves on the device. If you're signed into Game Center, your score also posts to the global leaderboard.

Apple Services

Sign in with Apple

Sign in with Apple is optional. WS Drift uses it only to label your local profile — we don't store your data on a server. You can hide your real email by tapping "Hide My Email" during the Apple Sign In flow.

Game Center

Game Center is operated by Apple, not Wyrick Studios. Manage your Game Center profile in Settings → Game Center on your device.
