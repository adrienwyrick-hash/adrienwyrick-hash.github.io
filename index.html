Step into the cockpit of a neon-trail starfighter and lock in for an arcade shooter built for one-handed thumb skill. WS Drift takes the classic Galaga-style squadron rush, throws in a modern drift-to-aim control scheme, and stacks it with 17 unlockable fighters across five tiers of power. Every round is a chain of procedurally generated formations — no two waves are ever quite the same.

Why WS Drift?

Because most arcade shooters fall into one of two camps: too easy and forgettable, or too punishing for a quick session. WS Drift sits exactly in the middle. Pick it up for a 90-second run before bed. Lose yourself for an hour chasing your daily streak. Climb the Game Center leaderboard with friends. The skill ceiling is high, but the skill floor is just one drag of your thumb.

No grind. No energy bars. No pay-to-win. Just you, your fighter, and the next wave.

🎮 How it plays

Drag anywhere on the screen to drift your fighter — your thumb stays where it lands, the ship reacts to your motion. Bolts fire automatically from your nose-cone, straight up. Slide sideways to put each enemy in your lane. The ones you don't shoot down loop back around for another pass.

Each round is a series of waves — squadrons that swoop, dive, and shoot back. Clear them all and the round advances. The next round throws denser formations at you, with more bosses, sharper dives, and tighter timing.

🎯 Three modes for three moods

🚀 Classic

Three lives. The full WS Drift experience — escalating wave count, every fighter unlocked at level 10. Perfect for marathon runs.

⏱️ Time Attack

One life. Faster pacing. No second chances. The mode for proving you've actually mastered the formations.

👑 Boss Rush

Two lives. Every single wave is all bosses. Highest possible points per kill — and the highest possible bullet count to dodge.

⚡ Features

  • 🌊Procedural waves — every formation is generated from a unique level + wave seed. No two runs play out the same.
  • 🎮One-handed drift control — drag anywhere, ship responds to motion. Thumb-friendly across iPhone and iPad.
  • 🔥Auto-fire bolts — focus on dodging and positioning. The shooting takes care of itself.
  • 🚀17 fighters — five tiers from free Standard up to Apex-tier Voidlord. One-time purchases.
  • 🎯3 modes — Classic, Time Attack, Boss Rush. Each pacing has its own leaderboard.
  • 🏆112 achievements — score, kills, rounds, ship mastery, mode wins.
  • 📅Daily play streak — keep the flame alive with a once-a-day visit. Reminder fires at 8pm if your streak is about to break.
  • 🎵Game Center leaderboards — see how you stack against the world.
  • 💎Free to play — no energy bars, no pay-to-win, no subscription. Optional Remove Ads for $2.99.
  • 🔒Privacy first — no server, no account required. Game Center is optional.
