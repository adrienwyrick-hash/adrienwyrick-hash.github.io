WS Drift

A wave-based arcade shooter

Pilot your fighter through wave after wave of swooping enemy formations. Drift, aim, survive. WS Drift is a retro-charged arcade shooter with modern controls and 17 unlockable ships, built for iPhone and iPad by Wyrick Studios.

Features

  • • Wave-based combat with procedurally generated formations
  • • 17 unlockable fighters across 5 tiers
  • • Classic, Time Attack, and Boss Rush modes
  • • Game Center leaderboard + 112 achievements
  • • Daily play streak with reminder notifications
  • • Free to play • Remove Ads available as a one-time purchase
