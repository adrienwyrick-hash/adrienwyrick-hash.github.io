Three modes

Pick your flavor.

Three pacing options, three separate leaderboards. Every fighter works in every mode. Switch whenever you want a different kind of run.

Mode 01 — Recommended

Classic

3 lives · Escalating waves · Marathon

The full WS Drift experience. Escalating wave count, every fighter unlocked at level 10. Perfect for long runs where you find your rhythm.

Mode 02 — Hardcore

Time Attack

1 life · Faster pacing · No mercy

One life. Faster pacing. No second chances. This is where you find out if you've really mastered the formations or just got lucky.

Mode 03 — Pure spectacle

Boss Rush

2 lives · Bosses only · Highest score

Every single wave is all bosses. Highest possible points per kill. Highest possible bullet count to dodge. You'll either flame out fast or set a record.
